Welcome to Japan!

Japan, an island nation in East Asia, blends ancient tradition with modernity. Renowned for its rich culture, including sushi, tea ceremonies, and cherry blossoms, Japan boasts advanced technology, stunning landscapes, and a strong sense of honor and respect.

Enoshima

Enoshima, a picturesque island near Tokyo, is famed for its scenic views and Shinto shrines. Accessible via the Enoshima Electric Railway, or "Enoden," this charming line offers a nostalgic journey along the coast, linking Enoshima to Fujisawa and Kamakura, showcasing seaside vistas and local culture.

Shibuya

Shibuya, Tokyo's vibrant district, pulsates with energy day and night. Famous for its bustling pedestrian scramble crossing, trendy fashion boutiques, and lively entertainment scene, Shibuya is a cultural hub where tradition meets modernity, attracting visitors from around the world.
